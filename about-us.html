About BubzLeague

Our Story, Mission, and Values

Our Story

Founded in 2015, BubzLeague began as a small gathering of alumni friends who wanted to keep their football passion alive after graduation. What started as casual weekend matches soon grew into a structured league with multiple teams and regular seasons.

Today, BubzLeague boasts 7 competitive teams, over 100 registered players, and a growing fan base. We've hosted 8 successful seasons, with each one more exciting than the last.

Our Mission

At BubzLeague, our mission is to foster camaraderie, sportsmanship, and healthy competition among alumni through the beautiful game of football.

We aim to:

  • Provide a competitive yet friendly football environment for alumni
  • Promote physical fitness and healthy lifestyles
  • Maintain and strengthen alumni connections
  • Develop football skills at all levels
  • Give back to the community through football-related initiatives

Our Core Values

Sportsmanship

We prioritize fair play, respect for opponents, and graciousness in both victory and defeat.

Community

We believe in the power of football to bring people together and strengthen alumni bonds.

Excellence

We strive for continuous improvement in our league organization and football skills.
