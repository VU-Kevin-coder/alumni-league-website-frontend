About John Doe

John Doe is the current top scorer in the BubzLeague with 8 goals in just 5 matches. Known for his clinical finishing and exceptional positioning, John has been instrumental in Stanley FC's unbeaten run this season.

Born in Kampala, John began his football career at the age of 12 with local academy Future Stars FC. His natural goal-scoring ability quickly became apparent, and he was signed by Stanley FC in 2018 as part of their inaugural squad.

Playing Style

A classic number 9, John excels at holding up play and bringing teammates into the game. His movement off the ball is exceptional, and he has developed a reputation for scoring crucial goals in big matches.
