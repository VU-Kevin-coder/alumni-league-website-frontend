Match Report

Stanley FC extended their lead at the top of the BubzLeague table with a hard-fought 2-1 victory over Rangers FC in a thrilling encounter at Alumni Stadium.

First Half

The match started at a frenetic pace with both teams looking to impose their style. Stanley controlled possession early but Rangers looked dangerous on the counter. The breakthrough came in the 23rd minute when John Doe curled a beautiful free-kick into the top corner from just outside the box.

Second Half

Stanley doubled their lead in the 68th minute with a well-worked team goal. Midfielder Alex Smith played a perfectly weighted through ball to John Doe who finished clinically past the Rangers keeper. Rangers pulled one back in the 75th minute through captain Michael Johnson, setting up a tense finale.

Final Thoughts

Stanley showed why they're league leaders with a composed performance, though Rangers made them work hard for the three points. The win extends Stanley's unbeaten run to five matches, while Rangers remain in fifth place.
